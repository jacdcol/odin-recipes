Cookies

Description

the best chocolate chip cookie recipe,
crisp edges and chewy middles every time or your money back
makes 24 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F // 175 degrees C
  2. cream together butter, white sugar, brown sugar until smooth
  3. beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla
  4. dissolve baking soda in hot water, add to batter along with salt
  5. stir in flour, chocolate chips, nuts
  6. drop in spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
  7. bake ~10min in preheated oven, until edges are nicely browned
