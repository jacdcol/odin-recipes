Jambalaya

Description

the perfect bowl of spicy cajun food

Ingredients

  • celery
  • onion
  • green bell pepper
  • jalepeno
  • cayenne
  • garlic
  • cajun/creole seasoning
  • bay leaf
  • thyme
  • protien(s) of choice (chicken, shrimp, andouille sausage, etc.)
  • chicken stock
  • crushed tomatoes
  • white rice
  • okra
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. saute the chicken and sausage
  2. saute the veggies (onion, bell pepper, celery, jalepeno, garlic)
  3. add rice, liquid, and seasonings
  4. cover and cook for 25-30 min, stirring ~ every 5 min
  5. add the okra, shrimp, chicken, sausage
  6. cook for 5 more min
  7. season to taste
  8. serve warm
