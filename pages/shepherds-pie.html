Shepherd's Pie

Description

Ireland's finest
a dish that is warm and loving, and always makes you feel at home

Ingredients

meat filling:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 lb. ground beef/lamb
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup frozen mixed peas and carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels

potato topping:

  • 3/2 - 2 lb. russet potatoes
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup half & half
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

meat filling:

  1. add oil to large skillet over medium-high heat for 2 min
  2. add onions to skillet and cook 5 min, stirring occasionally
  3. add ground beef/lamb (break apart with spoon), parsley, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper
    -stir well, cook 6-8 min until meat browned
  4. add worcestershire sauce and garlic, stir and cook 1 min
  5. add flour and tomato paste, stir until no clumps remain
  6. add broth, frozen vegetables - bring to boil, then simmer for 5 min
  7. set aside, preheat oven to 400 degrees F

potato topping:

  1. place potatoes in large pot and cover with water - bring to boil, then reduce to simmer for 10-15 min
  2. drain, and return potatoes to pot. let rest 1 min to evaporate remaining liquid
  3. add butter, half & half, garlic powder, salt, pepper. mash potatoes and mix ingredients together
  4. add parmesan cheese, and stir until combined

assemble casserole:

  1. pour meat filling into 9x9 or 7x11 baking dish
  2. spoon mashed potatoes on top, spread with spoon until even
  3. bake uncovered 25-30 min
  4. cool 15 min, then serve
